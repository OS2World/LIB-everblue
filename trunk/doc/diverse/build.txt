Hier nochmals die genaue Anleitung ;-)

install gcc v3.3.5 CSD 1
copy make.exe 3.79.1 into gcc bin directory
modify every eb_make.cmd to match path of gcc

install emx build environment (e.g. with UnixOS/2) and 
modify every eb_make2

call eb_make2 in UTIL\MAKEKEYS.EXE
   & copy ks_tables.h to X11.dll\XMISC
call eb_make2 in EMXHANDL.DLL
   & copy emxhandl.lib to X11.DLL

call eb_make in EBHOOK.DLL
   & copy ebhook.lib to XDAEMON.EXE, XDAEMON.DLL, X11.DLL
call eb_make in XDAEMON.DLL
   & copy xdaemon.lib to XDAEMON.EXE, X11.DLL
call eb_make in XDAEMON.EXE
call eb_make in X11.DLL\XCMS
call eb_make in X11.DLL\XLIBI18N
call eb_make in X11.DLL\XMISC
call eb_make in X11.DLL
